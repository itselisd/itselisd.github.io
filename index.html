About Elis

I'm Elis Dervishi, a former Chemistry student doing lab research, now a web developer and software engineer.

While I enjoyed the analytical aspects of science, I sought a field that allowed for more creativity. Having always been interested in technology, I started self-learning programming and discovered that coding came naturally to me. I've studied guitar and music from a young age and I think that practicing music developed my ability to hold mental models of intangible phenomena. The ability to focus on small musical details of a piece while keeping in mind the greater musical structure transfers over effectively to programming where it is important to focus on discrete portions of a project and work towards a larger codebase.

I decided to join a boot camp to accelerate my learning, which taught me how to build some fun projects with relevant modern technologies. I see myself combining the analytical skills and creative knowledge I have and bringing that into a technical context by being a developer. My expertise is in JavaScript, both in front and back end, but I am always looking to absorb more knowledge and currently delving into Python. I look forward to joining a dedicated, mid-size company as a junior developer.

When I'm not programming, I enjoy playing guitar and bass, composing songs, reading, travelling, and staying active.
