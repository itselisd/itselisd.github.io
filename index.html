About Me

I'm Elis Dervishi, a graduate in the field of Chemistry, now a web developer and software engineer.

While I enjoyed the analytical aspects of science, I sought a field that allowed for more creativity. Having always been interested in technology, I started self-learning programming and discovered a love and passion for building applications as well as learning about new technologies and challenging algorithms.

I decided to join a boot camp to accelerate my studies, where I learned to build some fun projects with relevant modern technologies. I see myself combining the analytical skills and creative knowledge I have and bringing them into a technical context by being a developer. My expertise is in JavaScript, both in front and back end, but I am always looking to absorb more knowledge and am currently delving into Python. I am passionate about writing code that is clean,efficient, reusable, and easy to debug and maintain.

When I'm not programming, I enjoy playing guitar and bass, composing songs, reading, travelling, and staying active. With my varied experience and enthusiasm for learning, I am confident I can be a strong asset to any development team. I look forward to joining a dedicated, mid-size company as a junior developer.
