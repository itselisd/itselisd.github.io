About Me

I am Elis Dervishi, Chemistry graduate, former web developer and now software engineer.

Having always had an affinity for technology, I started self-learning programming and tackling a variety of projects. I relish the creative opportunities programming provides by allowing me to break down complex problems and build creative solutions.
